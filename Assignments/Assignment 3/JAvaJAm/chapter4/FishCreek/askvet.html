Question: Our dog, Sparky, like to eat whatever the kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocolate.
Answer: Chocloate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog chocholate. Try playing a game with your children—when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
