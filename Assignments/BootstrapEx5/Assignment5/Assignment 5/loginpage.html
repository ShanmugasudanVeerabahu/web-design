International Student Assitance

The NEUISA (Northeastern University International Student) is a a platform for incoming international students to assist them in their early days assistance. Students can contact the volunteers from the University for pickup from the airport.

We also help them provide accomodation, provide information about opening of bank accounts, familiarize them with the happenings in and around the university, etc. To book travel plan and accomodation student need to SIgnb Up first from the link on right corner of this page!!

If you are already a student at Northeastern, you can help us by volunteering. Mail us at admin-isa@husky.neu.edu
